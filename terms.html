Self Drive Terms

The following additional terms apply specifically to self-drive vehicle rentals:

  • The renter must hold a valid Indian driving licence (minimum 1 year old) and be at least 21 years of age
  • A valid Aadhaar card or government-issued ID must be submitted at the time of vehicle handover
  • A refundable security deposit is required and will be held until the vehicle is returned in satisfactory condition
  • The renter is responsible for all fuel costs during the rental period
  • Any traffic violations, fines, or tolls incurred during the rental are the renter's responsibility
  • The vehicle must be returned in the same condition as received; any damage will be deducted from the security deposit
  • Sub-letting or transferring the vehicle to any other person is strictly prohibited
  • The vehicle must not be taken outside the agreed geographic boundaries without prior written consent
Accidents: In the event of an accident, the renter must immediately inform India by Car Driver and the local police. Failure to do so will result in forfeiture of the security deposit and possible liability for damages.
