About This Tour

The Golden Triangle is India's most celebrated road circuit — and for good reason. In just 6 days, you traverse three of the subcontinent's most captivating cities: Delhi, the pulsating capital where Mughal history meets modern India; Agra, home to the world's greatest monument to love — the Taj Mahal; and Jaipur, the Pink City of Rajput maharajas and bustling bazaars.

Travelling in a premium, air-conditioned vehicle with a verified local chauffeur who knows every shortcut, sunrise spot, and hidden gem along the way — this is how India is best experienced. No shared buses, no rigid schedules. Just you, the open road, and centuries of history unfolding at your pace.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

01
Arrival in Delhi
Day 1

Your driver will greet you at your arrival point in Delhi. After check-in and freshening up, enjoy an evening drive through the iconic India Gate, Rashtrapati Bhavan, and the illuminated Parliament House. A gentle introduction to the capital's grandeur.

India Gate Rashtrapati Bhavan Connaught Place
02
Old Delhi & New Delhi
Day 2

A full day exploring the contrasts of Delhi — the labyrinthine lanes of Chandni Chowk, the towering Jama Masjid, the spiritual calm of Humayun's Tomb, and the medieval majesty of Qutub Minar. End the day with the aromatic chaos of Dilli Haat.

Jama Masjid Qutub Minar Humayun's Tomb Chandni Chowk
03
Delhi → Agra (Taj Mahal)
Day 3 · ~200 km

Early morning departure to Agra via the Yamuna Expressway. Arrive in time for the Taj Mahal at its golden-hour best. Marvel at the world's greatest testament to love, then explore Agra Fort's towering ramparts and the majestic ghost city of Fatehpur Sikri en route.

Taj Mahal Agra Fort Mehtab Bagh Fatehpur Sikri
04
Agra → Jaipur
Day 4 · ~240 km

Morning drive through the countryside to the Pink City. Stop at the haunting Abhaneri Stepwells — one of India's most Instagram-worthy hidden gems. Arrive Jaipur by afternoon. Evening stroll through the glittering bazaars of Johari Bazar for gemstones and handicrafts.

Abhaneri Stepwells Johari Bazar Hawa Mahal (Evening)
05
Jaipur Sightseeing
Day 5 · Full Day

A full day in the Pink City. Begin with an elephant-blessed sunrise at Amber Fort, then glide through the City Palace's royal apartments. Stand before the Hawa Mahal's 953 latticed windows, visit the Jantar Mantar astronomical observatory, and round off with sundowners at Nahargarh Fort.

Amber Fort City Palace Hawa Mahal Nahargarh Fort Jantar Mantar
06
Departure / Return to Delhi
Day 6 · ~280 km

After a leisurely breakfast, your driver will transfer you to Jaipur Airport or Sanganer Railway Station for your onward journey — or drop you back in Delhi if preferred (4–5 hours via NH48). A comfortable end to a golden journey.

Jaipur Airport Drop Or Delhi Return

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to do the Golden Triangle tour?
October to March is the ideal time — pleasant temperatures (10–25°C) and clear skies perfect for sightseeing. Avoid May–June (extreme heat, 40–45°C) and monsoon months (July–September) when roads can be affected. Winter mornings in December–January can be foggy, which can affect the sunrise Taj Mahal experience.
Can I customise the itinerary or extend the tour?
Absolutely. This is a chauffeur-driven private tour — you have complete flexibility. Add days, change the pace, include side trips to Mathura-Vrindavan, Bharatpur Bird Sanctuary, or Ranthambore Tiger Reserve. Tell us your preferences and we'll tailor the trip accordingly at no extra planning fee.
Where does the tour start and end?
The tour typically starts and ends in Delhi (IGI Airport, railway station, or your hotel). However we can start from Jaipur, Agra, or any city along the route depending on your flight schedule. The return drop can also be at Jaipur airport if you're flying out from there.
What vehicle will be provided?
For this tour we typically provide a Toyota Innova Crysta or Tata Safari (7-seater, fully air-conditioned). For larger groups, we can arrange a Force Traveller. For couples or small groups preferring a sedan, the Honda City or Maruti Ciaz are excellent options at a lower price point.
Is hotel booking assistance available?
While hotel booking is not included in the base price, our team is happy to recommend trusted, well-located properties at every budget — from heritage havelis to boutique hotels — and assist with reservations on request. Just let us know your preference when you enquire.
